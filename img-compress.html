How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool makes it easy to reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  3. Adjust the compression level and resize options as needed
  4. Click the "Compress Image" button
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Your Images?

Image compression offers several benefits:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience and SEO
  • Save storage space: Reduce the amount of storage needed for your images
  • Bandwidth savings: Lower data usage when sharing or uploading images
  • Better performance: Optimized images improve overall website performance
